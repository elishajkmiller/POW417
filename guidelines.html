01

Registration

  • Teams must be registered before 3:00 PM on April 24, 2026.
  • Only one member from each team needs to register.
02

Opening Ceremonies

  • Opening ceremonies will be held on April 26, 2026 at 6:00 PM at 412 S Campbell Ave, Springfield, MO 65806.
  • The allotted 48 hours begin when the ceremonies are dismissed.
  • It is highly recommended that at least one member of each team attends the opening ceremonies.
  • The submission deadline will be posted on Instagram (@pow417films) and emailed to registered participants after the ceremonies for those unable to attend.
03

Participants

  • POW417 is open to any and all filmmakers, from amateurs to professionals.
  • Any person who contributes to the film in any way is considered a participant. If participants are added after registration, their names and roles must be listed in the submission.
  • Participants must volunteer their time — there may be no paid participants.
  • All participants must complete a Cast Release Form and/or Crew Release Form and submit it before the 48-hour deadline. (Included in the POW417 Release Form.)
  • Parental/guardian consent is required for participants under the age of 18. (Included in the POW417 Release Form.)
04

Pre-Challenge Prep

Prior to the opening ceremonies, teams may:

  • Organize team members (cast and crew)
  • Secure locations
  • Source music, sound effects, LUTs, equipment, props, costumes, and set pieces
05

Film Requirements

  • Must be entirely created during the allotted 48 hours.
  • Total runtime of five minutes (00:05:00:00) or less, including credits and OCC.
  • Must include an Opening Credit Card (OCC) at the beginning of the film.

06

Poster Requirements

  • Poster must be made entirely during the allotted 48 hours.
  • Must include the film title and your team name.
  • Must be submitted as a JPEG or PNG.
  • Resolution should be 1080 × 1920 pixels.
07

Equipment

  • Any type, brand, and number of video, film, or still cameras and sound equipment may be used.
08

Submissions

  • Films and posters must be submitted within the 48-hour time frame to be considered part of the challenge.
  • Films submitted late will still be screened following on-time entries but will not be considered official entries to POW417 2026.
  • To submit: upload your film to YouTube (Unlisted) and post the link along with your poster (JPEG or PNG) and release form (JPEG or PNG) to the submission portal at pow417.com.
  • If additional participants were added after registration, their names and roles must be listed in the submission.
  • Your release form must be completed prior to the deadline.
09

Screenings

  • Submitted films will be shown at a public screening (date and location TBD).
  • Films will be screened in the order that submission emails are received.
  • All completed films will be screened, but films submitted after the deadline will not be considered official entries.
  • Participants may not post their film in its entirety until after the public screening.
  • Posters, trailers, and other promotional materials may be posted prior to the screening.
10

Prohibited Actions

  • No audio or visual content may be recorded prior to the beginning of the allotted 48 hours.
  • No stock footage, pre-existing content, or A.I.-generated material may be used.
  • No paid participation — all team members must be volunteers.
  • A.I. may not be used in any capacity at any time during the production of your film.
  • No local, state, or federal laws may be broken during the production of your film.

Participation in prohibited actions and/or failure to adhere to the POW417 guidelines will result in disqualification and your film will not be screened.

By submitting your film, you consent to it being played at the POW417 2026 public screening event, uploaded to the POW417 website, and used in POW417 promotional material.

The rights and ownership of your film remain in your hands.
