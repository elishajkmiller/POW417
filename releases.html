Releases

Every person, place, and track in your film needs a release. Fill these out during production — don't wait until the deadline.

Required All applicable releases must be completed before your film is eligible for screening.

Not Sure Which Ones You Need?
If you shot on someone's property — location release. If someone is on screen — cast release. If anyone is under 18 — minor release. If there's music — music release. Everyone on your crew — crew release. When in doubt, fill it out.
