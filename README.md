# POW417 — Proof of Work

A 48-hour human film gathering in Springfield, MO. No AI. No rankings. Just crews, cameras, and the clock.

## What This Is

POW417 is an annual film challenge built around one rule: everything must be made by human hands. No AI-generated scripts, images, music, voice, or visual effects at any stage of production. Every team gets the same 48 hours and the same constraints.

## Registration

Registration form submissions are handled via Google Apps Script and written to a Google Sheet. The Apps Script endpoint URL is set in `register.html` under `APPS_SCRIPT_URL`.

## The Rules

1. **No AI** — Not even a little. No generative AI in any part of the pipeline.
2. **All footage shot in the 48** — Every frame captured during the official window. No stock footage.
3. **5 minutes maximum** — Credits included.
4. **Show up for the screening** — Being present is part of the deal.
